# SetSail: Modernizing Sales Performance

**Role:** Lead Product Designer  
**Timeline:** 3 Months  
**Outcome:** 40% increase in user engagement

## The Challenge
Sales teams lacked visibility into their performance data. The existing tools were clunky and disconnected.

## The Solution
We designed a unified dashboard that brings gamification and real-time metrics together.

### Key Features
1. **Real-time Leaderboards**: fostering healthy competition.
2. **Micro-incentives**: Rewarding small wins.
3. **Manager Insights**: Actionable coaching data.

## Results
- **40%** increase in daily active users.
- **15%** improvement in quota attainment.
